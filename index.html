History

In 1995, the Government of Bangladesh declared Sylhet as the sixth divisional headquarters of the country.Sylhet has played a vital role in the Bangladeshi economy. .... of Bangladesh's finance ministers have been Members of Parliament from the city of Sylhet.Badar Uddin Ahmed Kamran was a longtime mayor of Sylhet. Humayun Rashid Choudhury , a diplomat from Sylhet, served as President of the UN General Assembly and Speaker of the Bangladesh National Parliament.In 2001, the municipality was upgraded to the Sylhet City Corporation. It was made a metropolitan city in2009.

Culture

Sylheti culture comprises its cuisine, literature, language, songs, tradition etc. Other than Sylhetis, there are also people of.... Manipuri, Patra, Khasi, Chakma, tripuri and Santal ethnic groups in Sylhet. As a result, the language, history, heritage and culture of the region is quite diverse and rich. Sylheti culture is comparatively enriched with non-Aryan elements than Vedic features.The influence of Assam on the language and culture of Sylhet is noticeable as it was formerly a part of the State of Assam. It is said that after the Brahman War, the Manipuris came to Srihatta and Kachar and performed a dance called Lai offering God. Which is included in the ancient culture of this region. Later, dialectal Sanskrit was integrated into the ancient culture with the arrival of Arab, Turkish, Persian and other colonists including Aryans.
